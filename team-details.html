About the Teacher

Welcome! I'm William Samuel, a specialized English language teacher with over 10 years of experience in education. I hold a Master's degree in Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages (TESOL) from the University of Cambridge, and I currently work as a certified trainer at Nourhan Academy.

Throughout my professional career, I have helped hundreds of students improve their English language skills and achieve their academic and professional goals. I believe that effective education depends on creating an interactive and motivating learning environment, where the focus is on each student's individual needs.

I use the latest teaching methods and modern technologies in instruction, including project-based learning and interactive activities. My goal is to make learning English an enjoyable and fruitful experience for all my students.

Specializations & Skills

  • Teaching English for beginners and advanced learners
  • Preparing students for IELTS and TOEFL exams
  • Developing conversation and speaking skills
  • Teaching English grammar in a simplified way
  • Improving academic writing skills
